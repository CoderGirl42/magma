=== Notes and addendum to MobProgs for the Magma codebase

mprog_bribe_trigger
-------------------
For mprog_bribe_trigger, the amount of money is not in gold coins, but
rather in copper coins.  In order for bribes to be as you want them,
you may have to multiply the bribe amount by 100 (100 copper = 1 gold).
